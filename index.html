Department of Mathematics

The Department of Mathematics extends a platform for students to explore the nuances of mathematics beyond the traditional classroom setting. Here, academic knowledge intertwines with practical applications, allowing students to exhibit their mathematical talents. Through a range of activities and challenges, we encourage a deeper appreciation for the subject. Our commitment goes beyond textbooks, as we aim to cultivate a comprehensive understanding of mathematics. Join us in this journey of exploration, where students can delve into the intricacies of mathematical concepts and apply their skills in real-world scenarios. The Department of Mathematics is a space where learning transcends conventional boundaries.

About the Fest

Embark on an intellectual odyssey at Convergence, where the spotlight is on budding mathematicians! This inter-collegiate fest for undergraduates isn't just an event; it's a vibrant stage that pulses with the exchange of brilliant thoughts and innovative ideas. From thrilling competitions to engaging events, Convergence transforms the study of Mathematics into an interactive and entertaining affair. Since its inception, Convergence has been the heartbeat of aspiring mathematicians, providing a dynamic platform beyond the classroom walls. Join us for a celebration of knowledge, where passion meets competition, and where the future of mathematics unfolds in an atmosphere of intellectual camaraderie.

Math Tank

"MathTank" is a unique event that brings together mathematicians, researchers, and investors to explore groundbreaking mathematical ideas and their potential applications. Inspired by the popular TV show Shark Tank, MathTank provides a platform for individuals in the mathematical research field to pitch their innovative concepts and seek collaboration, investment, or partnerships to bring their ideas to fruition.
